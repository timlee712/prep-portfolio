ABOUT ME

Hello my name is Timothy Lee, I am a 20 year old student looking to step my toes into the world of coding and programming. My parents are from South Korea, and seeing them work so hard to provide for my family it really inspired me to do the same so that I can provide for them in the future.

I always loved computers and technology. I also liked building or fixing random things, I always built legos, built things in games as well. I have built my own furniture, my pc, and even my custom mechanical keyboard. Through my interest, I learned that coding could be a right path for me. And so far I am really enjoying it. For the past 2 years, I was struggling to find something I would be able to do as a career. But I am glad that I was able to find something I can try and enjoy at the same time.
